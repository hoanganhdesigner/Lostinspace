About me

I'm Hoang Anh, a Vietnamese Graphic Designer, Motion Designer, and 3D Designer working at Ho Chi Minh city.

Driven by my passion for design and motion. I try to learn and discover new things every day to create unique visual and experiences. Producing an idea and design without being confined to the conventional concept, widely groping various potentialities. I constantly seek to reach feelings and emotions by giving much importance to details.
